Automations & Technical Integrations

The most interesting problems I've solved are the ones where there was no existing playbook.

A lot of the work here involved connecting platforms that weren't designed to talk to each other, and building data flows where none existed before. That meant getting comfortable with ambiguity, teaching myself multiple coding languages along the way, reading documentation that wasn't written for marketers, and figuring it out mostly on my own until it worked.

What you'll find here is a cross-section of that: Journey Builder architectures, Automation Studio pipelines, custom Flowcode builds, and cross-platform integrations that didn't exist before. The screenshots show the builds. What they don't show is the gap that sparked the innovation.
