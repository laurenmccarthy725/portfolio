Automations & Technical Integrations

The most interesting problems I've solved are the ones where there wasn't a playbook.

Much of the work here involved connecting platforms that weren't designed to talk to each other and building data flows where none existed before. That meant getting comfortable with ambiguity, teaching myself new coding languages along the way, reading documentation that wasn't written for marketers, and experimenting until something worked.

Just as importantly, it meant collaborating closely with IT teams who understand the systems at a deeper level than I ever could. My role in those situations is often to explore what's possible, prototype solutions, and then work alongside technical partners to make sure what we build is stable, secure, and sustainable.

What you'll find here is a cross-section of that work: Journey Builder architectures, Automation Studio pipelines, custom Flowcode builds, and cross-platform integrations that didn't exist before.

The screenshots show the builds. What they don't show is the gap that sparked the innovation.

Journey Builder

Multi-Branch Holiday Engagement Journey

Large-scale holiday engagement journey built in Salesforce Marketing Cloud Journey Builder. Architected behavioral decision splits routing contacts based on open and click engagement, triggering product-specific email paths with timed sends across a multi-week campaign window.

Holiday Engagement Journey

Prior-year holiday journey architecture demonstrating iterative design evolution — built with cascading engagement splits to deliver up to 8 personalized send paths per contact based on real-time open and click behavior. Designed to simultaneously accommodate non-openers, single-touch openers, and highly engaged segments within a single journey framework.

Automation Studio

Welcome Series Automation Pipeline

Automation Studio pipeline supporting a brand welcome series — a scheduled 2-step automation running daily to query and inject new subscribers into the welcome journey. Configured for continuous execution with consistent completion windows, ensuring zero-gap subscriber onboarding.

Welcome Series Automation (Brand 2)

Second in a suite of parallel brand welcome automations — built and maintained simultaneously to support scaled subscriber onboarding across multiple brands without cross-contamination of audience data.

Welcome Series Automation (Brand 3)

Third parallel welcome journey automation, part of a coordinated multi-brand onboarding infrastructure. Daily scheduled execution with sub-minute completion windows reflects optimized query and send configuration.

Legacy Welcome Journey (Inherited & Updated)

Inherited and re-owned legacy Automation Studio workflow, updated and maintained to support a separate audience segment with its own welcome journey cadence — demonstrating cross-channel audience management across both consumer and professional practitioner databases.

Custom Pages

Giveaway Campaign Landing Page

Custom landing page built for a giveaway campaign — capturing contact information and social handle for contest entry with simultaneous email list acquisition. Integrated consent language aligned with corporate privacy policy and CAN-SPAM compliance requirements.

Mobile-optimized brand page built to bridge offline and online at the point of purchase. Integrated product imagery, video content, social links, and an email subscription CTA into a single post-scan experience — turning passive shelf browsers into an addressable digital audience.
