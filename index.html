Hi, I'm Lauren McCarthy
eCRM & Digital Manager

You've already seen my career and technical credentials in my resume — this page is about how and why I do the work.

My role lives at the intersection of marketing strategy, data infrastructure, and the people responsible for both. Over the past nine years, I've learned that the most valuable thing I bring to a team isn't a specific platform or toolset — it's the ability to sit in a room with an IT architect and a brand manager and make sure we're solving the same problem.

I’m often the person teams call when something breaks: a data flow stops syncing, an automation misfires, a campaign isn’t performing as expected, or an integration suddenly starts speaking the wrong language. I don’t just escalate the issue — I dig in until I understand what’s happening. I'm the first to say when something falls outside my immediate expertise, and the first to come back with answers, context, and a path forward.

The work you'll see throughout this site — dashboards, automation builds, campaigns, and integrations — is the visible output. But what I’m most proud of is what sits underneath it: the infrastructure that supports it, the cross-functional alignment that makes it possible, and the innovation that emerges when those pieces work together.

And yes — I built this site too.
