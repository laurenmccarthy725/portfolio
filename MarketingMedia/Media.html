Marketing Media

Technical depth and creative instinct don't often live in the same place. In my work, they've always existed side by side.

The projects here span campaign emails, nurture sequences, social content, sales enablement materials, and digital assets — all produced by me. Not directed. Not briefed out. Built.

But some of my favorite work happens when I'm collaborating with creative teams who bring a level of artistic vision I deeply respect. My role in those moments is making sure the technology, automation, and data infrastructure fully support the ideas they're bringing to life.

Because when the creative and technical sides of marketing live in separate rooms, strategy, execution, and measurement start to drift apart. I've always been happiest working in the space between them — helping great ideas move smoothly from concept to system to insight.
