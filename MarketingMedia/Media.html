Marketing Media

Technical depth and creative instinct don't often come together. For me, they always have.

The work here spans campaign emails, nurture sequences, social content, sales enablement materials, and digital assets — and I produced all of it myself. Not directed, not briefed out. Built.

That matters because it means the creative and the technical are never in separate rooms in my work. When I'm building an automation, I understand the email it's sending. When I'm pulling campaign performance data, I know exactly what the asset was trying to do. That end-to-end ownership is what keeps strategy, execution, and measurement from drifting apart.
